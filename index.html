About Me

I am an Associate Professor in the School of Computer Science at University of Birmingham in the UK as of 2024. My research interests focus on Artificial Intelligence for Computer Vision and Graphics, including 3D/4D contents representation/capturing/generation, 3D/4D digital human, image/video generative models, multi-modal foundation models, interpretability and reliability of AI models, etc.

Professional Services

  • Area Chair: ECCV 2024
  • Reviewer: CVPR (Outstanding Reviewer in 2019), ICCV, ECCV, SIGGRAPH, TPAMI, TIP
